overview

All That Remains is a first person narrative experience in which the player experiences the story through the characters memories. It is set in a possible near future where most people have left for a cleaner planet, and because of this consumption of the earths resources has skyrocketed. The few that stayed behind to live out their life are now dwindling, suffering a thick poisonous smog and rogue force send back to quell those that tried to fight against the careless destruction.
