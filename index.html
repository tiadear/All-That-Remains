overview of the game

John is one of the few left. He wanders through the city, suffering a thick poisonous smog, struggling with his memories of how he got here. Earth has been abandoned; humanity has escaped to a cleaner planet. The few that stayed behind are slowly dwindling in number, hiding from a military force who have been sent to Earth to quell those that try to fight against the careless destruction of the planet.

The player experiences the game through a first person narrative in which a series of flashbacks reveal how the Earth came to be this desolate wasteland. John's journey through past and present creates an emotional and memorable adventure game.
